Session 041 — 2026-03-10
Phase 1: Graphics Foundation (PLAN-UNIFIED Phase 1)

OBJECTIVE
Implement Phase 1 of the unified expansion plan: make the game visually stunning
with multi-depth ocean, detailed unit sprites, and enhanced particle effects.

CHANGES

1. Ocean Overhaul (Phase 1A)
   - Added 3 sea texture variants: deep ocean, coastal, shore
   - Water depth determined by counting adjacent land tiles (4-cardinal)
   - Shore foam overlay on water tiles with any adjacent land (8-neighbor)
   - Three-frequency wave animation (2.0 Hz + 1.3 Hz + 3.2 Hz shimmer)
   - Per-tile phase offset (col*0.4 + row*0.6) creates rolling wave pattern
   - Vertical tile bob ±1.2px — tiles physically rise and fall
   - Alpha oscillation ±15% for visible brightness ebb and flow
   - Foam scale breathing ±6% and alpha pulse 0.10–0.60
   - Vibrant blue color palette: brighter than original dark navy

2. Unit Graphics Revolution (Phase 1B)
   - All 9 unit types redrawn with detailed procedural sprites:
     Army: shield/helmet with emblem stripe
     Fighter: swept-wing jet with tail fin and cockpit
     Patrol: boat hull with radar mast/dish
     Destroyer: sleek warship with turret/barrel/bridge/mast
     Submarine: streamlined hull with conning tower/periscope
     Transport: wide cargo hull with 3 deck sections + bow ramp
     Carrier: flight deck with runway lines + island structure
     Battleship: heavy hull with dual turrets + superstructure
     Satellite: central body + dual solar panels + dish antenna
   - darken()/lighten() helpers for consistent color accents
   - Land tile texture gets subtle darker patches for variation

3. Selection & Polish (Phase 1C)
   - Selection glow: double-ring (outer dim + inner bright)
   - Multi-frequency pulse (4 Hz + 7 Hz) + scale breathing
   - Health bars: segmented per-HP, wider (22px), border outline
   - Larger shadows: 12×5 ellipse (was 10×4)
   - Explosions: 42 particles in 3 color layers + 8 lingering smoke
   - Smoke particles: negative gravity, scale expansion, 1.5s life
   - Death: 14 player-colored + 6 debris particles
   - Capture: 16-particle ring + upward sparkle burst
   - All particles get ±30% life variance for natural dissipation
