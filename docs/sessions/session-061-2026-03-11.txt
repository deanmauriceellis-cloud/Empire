### Game Integrity (17E)

## Key Findings
1. 2-player games end quickly (~turn 20-30 via AI surrender or elimination)
   → balance tests must account for short games
2. 6-player games last much longer (often 100+ turns without a winner)
3. Tribute floor-rounding issue: 30% of small incomes rounds to zero
4. Performance is excellent: all turns well under limits
5. Game is fully deterministic with seeded RNG
