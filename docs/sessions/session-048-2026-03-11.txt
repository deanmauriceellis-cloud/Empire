Session 048 — 2026-03-11
========================

## Summary
Design session: Kingdom MMO Master Plan. No code changes — pure architecture and planning.

## What Happened
1. User proposed expanding Empire Reborn into a persistent shared-world MMO:
   - Global map where all players play on a single map
   - Tick-based turns (1/minute or slower) with AI takeover for idle players
   - Players choose starting location and isolation level (ocean buffer width)
   - Kingdom-centric model with a "Crown City" capital
   - Monetization through cosmetics, VIP subscription, season pass

2. Analyzed current architecture for feasibility:
   - Identified all 2-player hardcoded assumptions (Owner enum, executeTurn signature,
     GameManager player slots, selectStartingCities, checkEndGame, AI surrender)
   - Confirmed game logic (combat, economy, tech, buildings) is owner-agnostic and scales
   - Confirmed networking and persistence layers are player-agnostic
   - Conclusion: HIGHLY FEASIBLE — refactoring, not redesign

3. Created PLAN-KINGDOM.md — comprehensive 17-phase master plan:
   - Phases 7-8: Finish gameplay foundation (bombard, AI economy) from PLAN-UNIFIED
   - Phases 9-11: Kingdom core (N-player, crown cities, tick server)
   - Phases 12-14: Persistent world (dynamic map, accounts, delta sync)
   - Phases 15-17: Monetization and polish (Stripe, cosmetics, balance, launch)
   - Estimated 13-22 sessions from current state

4. Key design decisions documented:
   - Crown City: sacred capital, lose it = lose kingdom (elimination first, vassalage later)
   - Territory: 4-tile radius from owned cities, grants vision and building rights
   - Isolation levels: Extreme (80-100 ocean), Far (50-70), Near (30-40), Center (10-20)
   - Shield: 2hr immunity after disconnect, rechargeable (1hr online = 1hr shield, 8hr max)
   - Monetization: cosmetics + VIP ($5/mo, 10% build speed) + season pass ($10/season)
   - Hard rule: NO pay-to-win (no stat boosts, no extra resources, no purchasable intel)
   - Spawn protection: 100 ticks for new players
   - Dynamic map: islands generated on player join, world expands as needed

5. Updated MEMORY.md with new plan reference and kingdom vision summary
