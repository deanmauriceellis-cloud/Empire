### AI Production Bug Fix (critical)
- `packages/shared/src/ai-production.ts:342`
  - Changed `minCommitWork` from `Math.max(5, ...)` to `Math.max(2, ...)`
  - ROOT CAUSE: Army buildTime=5, so max(5, ceil(5*0.25))=5, but city.work resets at 5
    → army cities could NEVER switch via ratio rebalance (work always 0-4 < 5)
  - Fix: max(2, ceil(buildTime*0.25)) gives armies threshold of 2 (reachable at work=2)

### Naval Production Ratio Rebalance

  - RATIO_4 (>30 cities): Full fleet ratios
